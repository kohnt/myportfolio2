Achievements

I have participated in many races and competitions. A large percentage of which are non-official and have no certificate

Drone Racing

I was influenced by my father and brother who flew Remote Controlled Planes. I began flying drones in September 2018. The Drones I have at home are all built by my family and I. Apart from building, I have also repaired and troublshoot them. I excel in Drone Racing, not only am I the top female pilot in Singapore, I am also well-known figure in my Drone Community. We meet up every weekend to engage in our hobby.

RC Planes

I started flying Remote Controlled(RC) planes in 2016, the RC Planes I have at home are all built by my family and I. Apart from building, I have also repaired and troublshoot them. My focus has been placed on Drone Racing and Technology as of early 2019 and endeavors in RC Plane building and flying has dwindled significantly.

3D Printing

My family started 3D printing through a SAFRA event in mid 2016, and bought our first printer through the course there too. I picked up 3D printing and designing after I entered drone flying as a hobby. We have a total of 7 Printers at home, 5 of which are consumer-level, 1 industry-level, and 1 entry-level printer. I 3D print accessories for my drones, gifts and leisure.
